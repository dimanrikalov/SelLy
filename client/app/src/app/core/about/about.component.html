  • About Us:

    SelLy was established in 2022 with the sole idea of contributing to reducing our waste and carbon footprint and stimulate the reusability of already existing goods. Our mission here at SelLy is to help everyone sell their little or completely unused possessions in order to limit material waste and reduce our carbon footprint. For every successful sale here, we at SelLy promise to plant a tree or donate up to 3% of the total price of the sold product. We are currently working closely with some of the biggest companies in the world to promote this new movement.
